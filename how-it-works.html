What You Get

  • Online booking page that clients can use 24/7
  • Customer list (simple CRM) to track clients and appointments
  • Automated confirmations and reminders
  • Basic follow-ups to reduce no-shows
  • Setup, training, and support to keep it simple

Step-by-step process

  1. Free consultation (15–20 min): we learn your services and workflow.
  2. Setup: we build your booking flow and your CRM structure.
  3. Testing: you book a few test appointments to confirm everything works.
  4. Launch: we publish and connect your notifications.
  5. Support: we help you adjust and improve as you start getting bookings.
