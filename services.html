👉 Our Core Service

Online Booking Website + CRM Setup

We provide a complete, all-in-one solution for local service businesses. We create a single professional booking website where your clients can book appointments online, and every booking is automatically connected to a simple CRM that helps you manage customers and schedules — all from one place.

1️⃣ What’s included

  • One-page professional booking website
  • Online appointment scheduling
  • Automatic confirmations and reminders
  • Calendar synchronization
  • Customer management system (CRM)
  • Basic automation for notifications and follow-ups

2️⃣ How it works

  • Your client visits your booking website
  • They choose a service and book an appointment
  • The appointment is saved automatically in the CRM
  • You manage everything from one place

3️⃣ Who this service is for

  • Hair salons and barbershops
  • Beauty and wellness businesses
  • Massage therapists and spas
  • Mechanics and service shops
  • Any appointment-based local business
